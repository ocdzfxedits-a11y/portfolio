About Me

Craft, rhythm,
and intention.

I'm Tanishq, a video editor with a passion for turning raw footage into stories that linger. My approach begins with the music — I build cuts around rhythm and emotion, letting sound shape the visual language of each piece.

From cinematic color grading to kinetic text effects, I blend technical precision with artistic instinct. Every project is a new conversation between picture, sound, and feeling.

01
Rhythm-First Editing Cuts are choreographed to music, breathing life into pacing that the viewer feels subconsciously.
02
Cinematic Color Language Color isn't correction — it's a narrative tool. Each grade is intentional, mood-driven, and distinctive.
03
Authentic Storytelling The best edits reveal what was already there. I preserve the genuine moments and amplify them.
